Simple Lemon-Herb Chicken

Home

Easy to make lemon-herb chicken dinner.

Simple, quick, and delicious meal.

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 15 mins
  • Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 (5 ounce) skinless, bonless chicken breast halves
  • 1 medium lemon, juiced, divided
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch dried oregano
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsely, for garnish

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Place chicken in a mixing bowl, pour 1/2 of the lemon juice over top with salt to taste.

  2. Step 2

    Heat oil in a small skillet over medium-low heat. Add chicken to oil, along with remaining lemon juice and oregano. Season with pepper. Saute until golden brown and the juices run clear, 5 - 10 mintues per side.

  3. Step 3

    Server with parsley for garnish.
